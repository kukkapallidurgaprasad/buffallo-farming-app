## 🌟 ANDHRA PRADESH BREEDS - COMPLETE DETAILS

### **1. Godavari Buffalo** 🆕
- **Origin:** Andhra Pradesh (Godavari Delta - East Godavari, West Godavari)
- **Color:** Black to greyish black
- **Milk Yield:** 7-10 liters/day
- **Fat Content:** 7-8%
- **Weight:** 400-550 kg
- **Key Features:** 
  - Well adapted to delta regions
  - Good milk yield for medium-sized breed
  - Heat tolerant
  - Thrives in coastal climate
  - Excellent for paddy field areas
- **Nickname:** Delta Pride
- **Best For:** Delta and coastal farming, paddy cultivation areas
- **Price Range:** ₹45,000 - ₹72,000

**Why This Breed?**
✅ Perfect for Godavari delta farmers
✅ Adapted to humid coastal climate
✅ Good milk production (7-10 L/day)
✅ Works well in waterlogged paddy fields
